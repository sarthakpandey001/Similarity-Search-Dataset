Uterine activity is assessed based on the number of contractions that are occurring in a 10 minute segment, averaged over a 30 minute period.Normal uterine activity is described as 5 or less contractions in a 10 minute segment, averaged over a 30 minute period. Excessive uterine activity is termed tachysystole and is described as more than 5 contractions in a 10 minute segment averaged over a 30 minute period.
